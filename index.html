Industrial-Grade Steel,
Every Category Covered

From structural beams to specialty sheets, our inventory covers every need for professionals who build with precision.

01 / HSS

Hollow Section

Square tubes, rectangular tubes, fasoon tubes, and welded pipes designed for structural supports and industrial use. Available in galvanized options for extended durability.

Square Tubes Rectangular Galvanized
02 / SHT

Sheets

Steel and specialty sheets including galvanized, pickled, corrugated, mesh, perforated, and Lexan varieties suitable for roofing and cladding projects.

Galvanized Corrugated Mesh
03 / PLT

Plates

Black plates and chequered plates for industrial floors, stairs, and heavy-duty platforms. Slip-resistant properties make them ideal for high-traffic environments.

Black Plates Chequered Heavy-Duty
04 / ROD

Rods

Circular, twisted, flat, and square rods suitable for grills, gates, and decorative ironwork. Precision-sized for seamless fabrication and professional finishing.

Circular Twisted Flat
05 / ANG

Angles

Equal steel angles providing uniform strength in all directions. Works well for frameworks, braces, and structural supports across commercial construction.

Equal Angles Structural Bracing
06 / BEM

Beams

I-Beams, H-Beams, and U-Channels designed for buildings, bridges, and industrial construction. High load-bearing capacity for the most demanding applications.

I-Beams H-Beams U-Channels
